Docura - Markdown Editor with Cloud Sync

Last Updated: January 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Docura ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our markdown editor application with optional Dropbox synchronization.

Key Point: Docura is a local-first application. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information or documents to our servers. All data processing happens locally on your computer.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Data

Docura stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Document Files: Your markdown files and their content
  • Application Settings: Your preferences (theme, editor settings, etc.)
  • Recent Files List: Paths to recently opened files and folders
  • Temporary Files: Auto-save and crash recovery data

2.2 Dropbox Integration (Optional)

If you choose to enable Dropbox synchronization, we store:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens for Dropbox API (stored locally, encrypted)
  • Sync Configuration: Folder mappings between your local system and Dropbox
  • Dropbox Account Email: Used only for display in settings
Important: Dropbox sync uses App Folder access. Docura can ONLY access files within /Apps/Docura/ folder in your Dropbox. We cannot access any other files or folders in your Dropbox account.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Document Content Display, edit, and save your work Local device only
App Settings Customize your experience Local device only
Dropbox Tokens Authenticate with Dropbox API Local device (encrypted)
Sync Configuration Map local folders to Dropbox Local device only

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Dropbox

When you enable Dropbox sync, your markdown files are transmitted directly from your device to Dropbox's servers using their official API. This communication is:

  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Direct: We do not relay or store your files on our servers
  • Limited: Only files you choose to sync are transmitted
  • App Folder only: Restricted to /Apps/Docura/ folder

Dropbox's handling of your data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

4.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share your data with any other third parties. We do not use:

  • ‚ùå Crash reporting services

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device in:

  • Linux: ~/.local/share/docura/
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/docura/
  • Windows: %APPDATA%\docura\

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encrypted Tokens: Dropbox OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Secure Communication: All API calls use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't operate servers or cloud storage
  • Local Processing: All document processing happens on your device

6. Your Rights and Control

6.1 Access and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: All data is stored in standard formats (JSON, plain text)
  • Export Data: Use File ‚Üí Export to save in various formats
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Revoke Dropbox: Click "Disconnect" in settings or revoke in Dropbox console

6.2 Dropbox Permissions

You can manage Dropbox permissions at any time:

  • Revoke access in Dropbox Connected Apps
  • Delete /Apps/Docura/ folder to remove synced data
  • Disconnect in Docura settings (Settings ‚Üí Cloud Sync ‚Üí Disconnect)

7. Children's Privacy

Docura does not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general use and does not require age verification.

8. International Users

Docura is a local-first application that works entirely on your device. If you use Dropbox sync, your data may be transferred to Dropbox's servers, which may be located in various countries. Please refer to Dropbox's Privacy Policy for information about international data transfers.
